About Wanderlust

We're passionate about creating unforgettable travel experiences for adventurers around the world.

Our Story

From a small dream to a global travel company

2023

Our Beginning

Wanderlust was founded by a group of passionate travelers who wanted to share their love for exploration with others. Starting with just 3 employees, we organized small group tours to Southeast Asia.

2023

Expanding Horizons

After successful tours in Southeast Asia, we expanded our destinations to include Europe and South America. Our team grew to 15 dedicated travel experts.

2024

Digital Transformation

We launched our online booking platform, making it easier for travelers to discover and book their dream vacations. This innovation helped us reach a global audience.

2025

Where We Are Today

Today, Wanderlust offers tours to over 50 countries across 6 continents. We've helped more than 10,000 travelers create unforgettable memories, and we continue to grow with a focus on sustainable and authentic travel experiences.
