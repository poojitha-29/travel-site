Featured Package: Greek Island Explorer

Experience the best of Greece with our most popular package

Our Greek Island Explorer package takes you on an unforgettable journey through Athens, Santorini, and Mykonos. Experience the perfect blend of ancient history, stunning landscapes, and vibrant island culture.

Sample Itinerary

  • Day 1-2: Athens
    Arrive in Athens and explore the iconic Acropolis, Parthenon, and historical Plaka district. Enjoy traditional Greek cuisine and immerse yourself in the city's rich history.
  • Day 3-5: Santorini
    Ferry to Santorini and marvel at the stunning caldera views. Explore the white-washed villages of Oia and Fira, relax on volcanic beaches, and experience a breathtaking sunset cruise.
  • Day 6-7: Mykonos
    Travel to Mykonos and discover its famous windmills and Little Venice. Enjoy the island's renowned beaches and vibrant nightlife. Optional day trip to the ancient ruins of Delos.
  • Day 8: Departure
    Return to Athens for your departure flight, taking with you unforgettable memories of the Greek Islands.
