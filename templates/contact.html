Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I book a trip?

You can book a trip by browsing our destinations, selecting your preferred package, and following the booking process on our website. Alternatively, you can contact our travel experts who will assist you with the booking process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. For certain destinations, we also offer payment plans.

Can I customize a tour package?

Yes, we offer customization options for most of our tour packages. Please contact our travel consultants with your specific requirements, and we'll create a personalized itinerary for you.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the package and destination. Generally, cancellations made 30 days or more before departure receive a full refund minus a small administrative fee. For detailed information, please refer to the terms and conditions of your specific booking.

Do you offer travel insurance?

While we don't provide travel insurance directly, we strongly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance for your trip. We can suggest reliable insurance providers that offer coverage suitable for your journey.
